REVIEW

Stereoscope Coffee in Buena Park is a well established coffee shop in LA. They recently started selling matcha and it deffinitly lived up to their standards. I was really impressed by the complexity of the flavor. Unlike other places, they offer different variants of matcha, which allows you to select specific notes that you prefer, making the experience more personalized. The matcha I had had a deeper, richer taste. However, my cousin, who’s new to matcha, found the flavors a bit too strange. He mentioned that it felt a little too unusual for his taste, but he thought that if he drank matcha more often he could see himself getting used to it. Overall, Stereoscope offers a more refined matcha experience, but it might be a bit too complex for someone who’s just starting out with matcha.
