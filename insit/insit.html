WHAT IS IN-SĪT?

Insit Matcha in Buena Park is a sit in cafe that spcializes in coffee and matcha tonics!

TASTE

The flavor didn’t quite hit the mark for me and it tasted more like green tea ice cream than the rich, earthy matcha I was hoping for. It felt overly sweet and lacked the depth of flavor that I typically enjoy in a good matcha. It almost had a dessert-like quality, which might appeal to some, but it wasn’t what I was looking for in a matcha drink.

FINAL THOUGHTS

Overall, I didn’t find it to be as satisfying or authentic as I’d hoped, but it could be a good option for those who enjoy sweeter, ice cream-like matcha flavors.
